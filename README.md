# рҹ“Ң Business Objectives

- Analyze historical insurance policy data to identify claim patterns.
- Identify high-risk customer segments based on demographics and policy characteristics.
- Evaluate vehicle-related factors influencing insurance claims.
- Measure the impact of safety features on claim occurrence.
- Analyze regional and policy-based claim trends.
- Develop interactive Power BI dashboards for business monitoring.
- Generate actionable business insights to improve underwriting and premium pricing.
